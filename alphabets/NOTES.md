## Terminology

Use Upcase, Downcase AND Titlecase (!)

- Example: Ö  -> Upcase: OE, Downcase: oe, Titlecase: Oe (!)
- Example: Æ  -> Upcase: AE, Downcase: ae, Titlecase: Ae (!)
